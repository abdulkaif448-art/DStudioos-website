Our Story

Born from a love of light, culture, and human connection.

Founder and creative director Dia Sharma launched DStudioos after a decade of leading luxury brand campaigns. Today we are a collective of photographers, directors, editors, and stylists shaping refined narratives for modern couples.

Studio Values

We believe photographs should feel alive decades from now. Every commission is guided by:

  • Intentional storytelling
  • Respect for culture & tradition
  • Effortless luxury
  • Kind collaboration
